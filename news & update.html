Welcome to news and update

Ongoing Project

Several ongoing development projects are taking place in the Sungai Buloh area. A total of 37 development projects have been implemented in the Sungai Buloh parliamentary constituency, including property developments such as D'sara Sentral, Saujana Perdana, Hemingway Residences, Hampton Residences, Joya@Gamuda Gardens, and Amverton Hills

Additionally, the projects include upgrades to public utilities, mosques and surau, non-Muslim places of worship, national schools, secondary schools, and vernacular schools

Community achievement

The area is described as an upcoming neighborhood with secure living experiences, family-friendly commercial areas, and various property launches.In terms of community achievements, there have been several initiatives to improve the quality of life in the area, including the establishment of sensory centers for the disabled community, public libraries, and activity centers for senior citizens.

Additionally, there have been efforts to increase access to childcare centers for the B40 community, establish job exchange programs, and provide internship programs for students with disabilities

Volunteer

Sungei Buloh Volunteers: The Sungei Buloh Volunteers is a group of dedicated and enthusiastic volunteers with a passion for conserving the natural heritage and native flora and fauna of the Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve. They organize regular volunteer activities and welcome new members to join their cause

A Walk in Nature: Youth Corps Singapore organizes a volunteer event called "A Walk in Nature" at the Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve. Participants can join a guided walk and learn about the flora and fauna of the reserve while helping to maintain the trails and habitats
