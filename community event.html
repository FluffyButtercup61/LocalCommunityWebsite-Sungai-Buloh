Welcome to Sungai Buloh Community Event!

Event Details:

Join us for an unforgettable day of community spirit and celebration at the Sungai Buloh Community Event! Taking place on February 2, 2024, at The Store Sungai Buloh Mall, our event promises a day filled with fun, laughter, and connection.

Schedule of Activities:

Explore the exciting lineup of activities we have planned for you. From cultural performances that showcase the diversity of Sungai Buloh to interactive workshops and games for all ages, there's something for everyone. Check out the detailed schedule to plan your day.

Community Engagement:

This event is all about bringing the Sungai Buloh community together. Engage with your neighbors, make new friends, and strengthen the bonds that make our town special. Participate in group activities and share your experiences with fellow community members.

Volunteer Opportunities:

Interested in getting involved? Learn about volunteer opportunities available during the event. Whether you want to assist with setup, help run activities, or contribute in any way, your participation is valued and makes a positive impact on our community.

Interactive Map:

Navigate the event seamlessly with our interactive map. Find key areas such as activity zones, food stalls, and rest areas. Make the most of your day by exploring all the event has to offer.

Photo Opportunities:

Capture the moments of joy and connection throughout the day. Share your photos on social media using our event hashtag [#SungaiBulohCommunityEvent] and be part of the online conversation. We love seeing our community come together!

Event Feedback:

Your feedback matters! Share your thoughts about the event, what you enjoyed, and any suggestions for improvement. Your input helps us make future community events even more enjoyable for everyone.

COVID-19 Guidelines:

Your safety is our priority. Find information about the COVID-19 safety measures we've implemented to ensure a secure and enjoyable event experience. Let's celebrate responsibly and take care of each other.

Join Us for a Day of Community Unity!

Sungai Buloh Community Event is a day to celebrate the vibrant spirit and togetherness of our community. We look forward to seeing you there and creating lasting memories together.
